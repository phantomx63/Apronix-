🤖 Apronix

A Powerful Multipurpose Bot for Discord

🌟 Overview

Apronix is an advanced Discord bot packed with features to make your server powerful, fun, and well-managed. Whether you need moderation, music, utility tools, or entertainment commands — Apronix does it all with style.

⚙️ Features

  • 🛡️ Powerful moderation commands (ban, mute, warn, purge)
  • 🎶 High-quality music system with filters and playlists
  • 🎉 Giveaways, level system, and auto-moderation
  • 🔔 Welcome, logging, and custom prefix setup
  • 💎 Fast and optimized with modern Discord.js framework

🎵 Commands

  • /play – Play songs from YouTube or Spotify
  • /skip – Skip current track
  • /pause, /resume – Control playback
  • /queue – View or manage queue
  • /ban, /unban, /warn – Manage members
  • /userinfo, /serverinfo – View info

💠 Premium Features

  • 🎧 320kbps Audio Quality
  • 🚀 Faster response speed
  • ✨ Exclusive music filters and effects
  • 💬 Priority support access
